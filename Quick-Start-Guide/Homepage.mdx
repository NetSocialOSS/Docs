## Welcome to the Home Page!

Hello again, curious traveler! It looks like you have completed [Step 2](/Quick-Start-Guide/Login). Congrats!

<Tip>You can also browse the Home page without logging in!</Tip>

Now that you're on our [Home Page](https://netsocial.app/home), let’s explore all the things you can do!

If you see any images or GIFs that you want to enlarge for a better look, simply click on them! This will allow you to see the content in greater detail.

### Sorting Posts by Coteries

Wow! It seems you’ve already figured out how to join a coterie before we even told you—impressive! Now, let’s sort posts according to coteries. Click the **Select Coteries** button, and a dropdown menu will appear. From there, you can choose the coterie from which you want to see posts. Easy, right?

Want to see a post in all its glory? Click the **comment button** on the post. This will redirect you to a page where you can see all the details, including comments and author information (who posted it).
